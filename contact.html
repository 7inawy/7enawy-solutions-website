Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a typical project take to complete?

Project timelines vary depending on complexity and scope. Simple websites typically take 2-4 weeks, while complex web applications or mobile apps can take 3-6 months. We provide detailed timelines during our initial consultation based on your specific requirements.

Do you provide ongoing maintenance and support?

Yes, we offer comprehensive maintenance and support packages for all our projects. This includes regular updates, security patches, performance monitoring, and technical support. We believe in long-term partnerships with our clients.

What is your development process like?

Our process follows four main phases: Discovery (requirements gathering and planning), Design (UI/UX and system architecture), Development (coding and testing), and Deployment (launch and optimization). We maintain transparent communication throughout each phase and provide regular updates.

Can you work with our existing team or systems?

Absolutely! We frequently collaborate with existing development teams and integrate with current systems. Whether you need additional expertise, specific technical skills, or help scaling your team, we can adapt our approach to complement your existing resources.
